What is the Box Model?

The box model is comprised of properties that define elements that take up space on a webpage. In other words, its how to organize your elements so they aren't all clunky and one liners. Elements are comprised of a few things;

  • Areas size
    • Height and Width of the content area.
  • Padding
    • Amount of space between content area and border.
  • Border
    • Thickness and style of border surrounding content area and padding.
  • Margin
    • Amount of space between border and the outside edge of an element.
